### About

Lazygitrs is my Rust rewrite of lazygit. It starts about **3.24x faster** than the original and adds a few things I wanted:

- **AI-generated commits** — hooks into Claude, OpenAI, or other models for conventional commit messages
- **Side-by-side diffs** with syntax highlighting and vim-style navigation
- **GitHub shortcuts** — copy repo URLs, open PRs, manage things without leaving the terminal
- **30+ themes** — Catppuccin, Dracula, Tokyo Night, etc. with an interactive picker
- **Interactive rebasing** — pick, squash, fixup with a proper UI
- **Commit comparison** — visualize differences between commits or branches
