### About

Bagon Hooks is a port of Mantine's hooks to SolidJS. I kept reaching for Mantine hooks when working in SolidJS and they just didn't exist, so I made them. 40+ hooks covering DOM interaction, state management, and browser APIs.

It wasn't a 1:1 copy — I had to remap a lot of Mantine's imperative patterns to work with SolidJS's reactive model. Some hooks like `useDisclosureData` got reworked to better fit SolidJS's granular reactivity.

- **Zero dependencies** — only needs SolidJS as a peer
- **Tree-shakable** so you only ship what you use
- **Full TypeScript support**
