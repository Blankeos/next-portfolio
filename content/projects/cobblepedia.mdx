### About

Cobblepedia is a fast, keyboard-first encyclopedia for Cobblemon — a Pokémon mod for Minecraft. I play a lot of Cobblemon and got tired of digging through wikis, so I built something where you just hit Cmd+K, type, and get instant results.

- **Instant search** with live preview
- **Smart filtering** — type modifiers like "moves", "spawn", "evolution" to narrow results
- **Sprites, artwork, and 3D models** included
- **Cobbleverse support** — works with community addon packs like AllTheMons and Mega Showdown
- **Data from multiple sources** — official mod data, PokeAPI, and community contributions
